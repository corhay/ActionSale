Welcome to ActionSale's API

This endpoint serves data relative to discounts from Migros, Coop and Denner

Usage:

/v1/discounts/all

Paramaters:
  • search

    • Returns products whose title contains at least one of the passed keywords

  • date

    • Returns products available at a certain date | Format: dd.mm.yyyy

  • store

    • Returns products from the given store(s) | Options: mig, denn, coop

Example:

/v1/discounts/all?search=pizza+beer&date=21.05.2020&store=coop+denn

The request above will return all products that contain either 'pizza' or 'beer' in their title that were available on the 21st of May 2020 from Coop and Denner
